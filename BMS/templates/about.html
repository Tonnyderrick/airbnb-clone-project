About Us

Our Mission

At MyScholar KE, we aim to provide students in Kenya with a seamless and efficient way to apply for bursaries and scholarships, ensuring that financial barriers do not hinder academic success.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of educators, developers, and designers committed to empowering students with the resources they need to succeed.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform in Kenya for scholarship and bursary access, contributing to the development of future leaders.
